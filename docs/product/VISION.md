# TokToTable --- Vision

TokToTable exists to bring **calm and structure** to the moment after
food inspiration.

People don't need more recipes. They need a reliable way to turn ideas
into groceries.

## What TokToTable Is

-   A weekly shopping companion
-   A personal food admin tool
-   Calm, predictable, and explicit

## What TokToTable Is Not

-   A social network
-   A creator platform
-   An AI playground
-   A grocery marketplace

## Design Philosophy

-   Local-first
-   No silent automation
-   User decides, system assists
-   Stability before growth

## North Star

> "I use this every week without thinking."
